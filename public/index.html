FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

First FAQ question

I can't verify my phone number.How?

  • You can only sucessfully verify a phone number you own.
  • Your phone number must be able to recieve phone calls and SMS messages to be verified.
  • You must have Internet Connection through mobile data or Wi-Fi. Verification may not work if you have bad Internet Connection.

    • How to verify?

    1. Enter your phone number
      • Enter your phone number correctly. Do not start with an 0 before your phone number.
    2. Tap Next to request a code.
    3. Enter the 6-digits code you recieve via SMS.

    If you still didnt recieve the 6-digits code

    • Wait for sometime and retry. The wait might take approximately 5-10 minutes.
    • Do not guess the 6-digits code. Wait patiently for the code.

Updating Elli.inc

You can easily update Elli incorporated from your phone's application store. Search for Elli.inc app and tap Update

We encourage all our users to always use the lastest version of Elli.inc

Note: Lastest versions contain the newest features and fixed bugs.

How long does it take for my order to get delivered?

Delivery time varies from all Elli Assistants. It also depends on the number of orders one has. If one has more deliveries to make, wait patiently to receive your delivery.

How much money can I earn?

With Elli, you can earn good money depending on the type of deliveries you make per the distance and time of your delivery. The faster your delivery, the more you earn. Just do it Elli.

Second FAQ question

How do I place an order?

  • Tap the Order button from the list of menus below.
  • Tap Select a place to order from.
  • Select your current location from the map.
  • Pick a Delivery Location.
  • Enter whatever you want from this location with all neceassary details.
  • Place your order.

Third FAQ question

I need to cancel my order! How can I do this?

  • Tap My Requests button to access your pending order.
  • Tap the Remove option to remove the order.

Fourth FAQ question

How can I view my Order?

  • Tap My Requests button to access your pending order.
  • Tap the View option.

Fifth FAQ question

Where is my Order?

After you place any order, all Elli users in the radius of your specified location will be notified of your order.

Anyone in that radius can accept your order, purchase anything you need and deliver it right at your dooratep.

Sixth FAQ question

I do not reicecve any notifications. How?

You can change your configurations on notifications in your phone's settings.

Phones Notification Settings

Go to your phone's Settings > Apps & Notifications > Elli > Notifications. On most phones, you will have the option to;

  • Block all notifications from this app.
  • Allow the app's notifications be heard when Do not Disturb mode is set to Priority mode only.
  • Allow this app to emphasize certain notifications by sliding them briefly into view on the current screen.

You can also navigate phone's Settings app > Sound & Notifications to adjust notificatons volume.
